# Authors

Trix is primarily developed and maintained by:

- **Mark Guidarelli** <guido@grumpy-cat.com>

The name is a nod to the author's cat.  The `(_)` eye and `>·<`
whiskered nose in the logo are an homage to her.  Trixie is the cat
in concatenative.

External contributors are listed in the git history; see
`git shortlog -sn` for a current roster.  New contributions are
welcome -- see [CONTRIBUTING.md](CONTRIBUTING.md) for the workflow.
